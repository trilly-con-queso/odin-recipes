Make You Pray Matar Paneer

Description

Yet another great vegetarian recipe. This one will lead you to spiritual enlightenment.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • 10 to 12 cashews
  • 1 teaspoon chopped green chilies or serrano peppers

Steps

  1. Add 3 tablespoons oil in a 2-litre pressure cooker. Let the oil become hot. Reduce heat to a medium-low heat. Add ½ teaspoon cumin seeds and fry until they splutter.
  2. Add in your ground masala paste, and mix well.
  3. Now you will sauté the paste mixture for about 10 to 12 minutes on a medium-low heat.
  4. After sautéing the masala paste, add in all of the dry spice powders, stir to combine well and sauté for a minute.
